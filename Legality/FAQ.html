Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Music Judger?

Music Judger is a platform that allows music lovers to rate and review songs, albums, and artists. You can discover new music and share your opinions.

2. How do I log in to Music Judger?

You can log in using your Spotify account. Simply click the “Log in” button on the homepage, and you will be redirected to Spotify’s login page. Once logged in, you can rate songs, albums, and artists and explore your personalized music recommendations.

3. Why do I need a Spotify account to use this site?

We use your Spotify account to:

  • Personalize your experience by suggesting music based on your listening history.
  • Provide a seamless way to log in without creating a separate account.

Note: Your data is only used according to our Privacy Policy and Spotify’s Privacy Policy.

4. What data does Music Judger access from my Spotify account?

When you log in with your Spotify account, we may access the following information:

  • Your Spotify username
  • Your public playlists
  • Your recently played songs and albums

This data is used to enhance your experience on our site, such as suggesting relevant music to rate or linking your ratings to your Spotify profile. We do not store or sell any of your data.

5. Is my Spotify account information safe?

Yes! We use Spotify’s OAuth login system, which ensures that your Spotify credentials are never shared with us. You are always redirected to Spotify’s secure login page, where Spotify handles your login information directly. We only access the data you grant permission for when you log in.

6. Can I use Music Judger without logging in with Spotify?

No, you must log in using a Spotify account to use the features of our site. Logging in with Spotify allows us to offer a personalized experience and ensure that your ratings and reviews are associated with your account.

7. How do I rate songs, albums, or artists on the site?

Once logged in, you can browse our collection of songs, albums, and artists. To leave a rating, simply:

  • Select a song, album, or artist you want to rate.
  • Choose a rating (e.g., stars).
  • Submit your rating and review.
